I am a self-taught developer looking to get a start in the industry. I work hard and enjoy being part of a team.

Family Recipes

A recipe site my brother asked me to make for my mom for christmas 2023. He wanted a simple, easy to use site for my mom and others in the family to access our collection of recipes. She cried when we showed it to her, so we considered that a success!

CRONbook

A simplified 'facebook' based on my housing group from college. Our name was Sigma Phi Omicron but, we called ourselves 'CRONS' for short, hence 'CRONbook'. This site was a project to practice building a site from the ground up, including models, views, controllers, and data management.

Chess

A terminal based chess game written in ruby. This was one of my favorite projects because of the increasingly intricate classes needed to represent the pieces, and the sense of accomplishment when it finally came together. Unfortunately, I am still not very good at actually playing chess...

Rock, Paper, Scissors

A simple RPS game played in the browser. I love to add fun touches to everything I do, and here it took the form of unnecessarily descriptive feedback. But I am also an aspiring writer, and need to get practice in wherever I can!

Calculator

An ode to my mathematics background. I had a lot of fun learning how to integrate mathematical concepts into the world of coding. One of my first projects but still one of my favorite.

Etch-a-Sketch

When The Odin Project assinged this project I was still a baby coder and it absolutely amazed me how much could be accomplished with a little JS. Little did I know how little I knew, but this project still reminds that no matter how much I understand, more is always possible.
